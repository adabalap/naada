About
Naada · నాద
Primordial sound, reimagined
Personal Edition

An ad-free, 320 kbps Indian-music player that installs to your home screen and runs from your own phone. No accounts, no tracking, no ads — built for one listener, engineered like a small production system.

What makes it special
  • Audio streams directly from the CDN — your phone server only handles metadata.
  • Synced, line-by-line lyrics that scroll with the song.
  • AI story cards & trivia about the films and composers you listen to.
  • Full library, playlists & queue editing — all stored privately on-device.
  • Lock-screen controls & album art via the Media Session API.
  • Installable PWA with offline app shell and instant updates.
Privacy by design

No sign-in, no analytics, no third-party tracking. Your liked songs, playlists and history never leave this phone. AI features are optional and use a key kept off the browser entirely.

Under the hood

Vanilla JS PWA + a lightweight Flask service on Termux. DES-ECB stream decryption, multi-source lyrics, Gemini Flash AI with model fallback, rotating logs, and graceful degradation throughout.

Your data

Your library, playlists, liked songs and settings live on this device. Back them up to a file so they're safe across updates, reinstalls, or a new phone.
